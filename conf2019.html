February 10, 2019

Social x Design

We are more connected than ever before — but are we moving toward one another, or retreating inward? This February, xDesign will convene a community of social animals. We’ll explore the ways design shapes how we relate in heart, in mind, and in body. And we don’t expect clear-cut answers.In heart, how do our networked lives drive connection and disconnection?In mind, how does ever-increasing intelligence drive trust and mistrust? In body, how do redefined spaces create new forms of assembly and disassembly? Let’s get social — by design.

Sponsored by J.W. Kaempfer, Jr. (MBA ‘71), Harpoon Brewery, and Lyft
