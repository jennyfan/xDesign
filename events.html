Workshop

"The Workshop Workshop": How to Run Creative Workshops

Knowing how to set up and run effective creative workshops is rarely taught in school and yet might be one of the highest-impact, lowest-effort skills you can learn to make you a better designer/manager. Benjamin Shown from Tectonic Design Studio will teach a format and set of exercises to get disparate creative ideas aligned, set expectations, build trust, and identify the foundational elements of whatever you’ll be building as a team.

Lecture

Poetic Kinetics: Patrick Shearn, Installation Artist

Patrick Shearn is an LA-based artist specializing in larger-than-life, immersive public art installations. Shearn’s expertise in animatronics, robotics and visual effects has lent traction to a prolific career as creative director for his studio Poetic Kinetics. Shearn will walk us through his unconventional career trajectory, offering a behind-the-scenes look at how his art comes to fruition and reimagines the landscapes it inhabits.
