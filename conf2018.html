February 23-24, 2018

Influence x Design

To design is to influence the experience of individuals, the fabric of communities, the structure of organizations, and the dynamics of systems. At the sixth-annual HarvardxDesign conference, we will convene today’s leading thinkers and doers to explore design’s influence across these four scales.

Sponsored by J.W. Kaempfer, Jr. (MBA ‘71), Cognizant, Harpoon Brewery, and McKinsey
