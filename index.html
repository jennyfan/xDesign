HarvardxDesign

xDesign is a collaborative organization among groups at the Harvard Graduate School of Design, Harvard Business School, Harvard College, and other organizations from across the Harvard community.

It aims to leverage expertise within the University by bringing together creative thinkers, design luminaries, professors, experts from various backgrounds, and students to engage, debate, and reinterpret the design process.

By promoting a culture of collaboration, we strive to address contemporary global problems with cross-disciplinary thought and empower students to operate in areas beyond the traditional boundaries of architecture and design.

xDesign Conferences

The xD conference is a collaborative event between HBS and the GSD with a constantly evolving structure, experience, theme, and speakers coming from industry, academic institutions, governments, and more. Each year, we learn from the last and iterate with an ambition to make the conference provocative, unique, and fun!

Continuum handles whitespace innovation, experience strategy, product & service design, and more. In the 60-75 minute visit, they will give us an office tour, tell us about what they do, and generally introduce design careers.

Workshop

"The Workshop Workshop": How to Run Creative Workshops

Knowing how to set up and run effective creative workshops is rarely taught in school and yet might be one of the highest-impact, lowest-effort skills you can learn to make you a better designer/manager. Benjamin Shown from Tectonic Design Studio will teach a format and set of exercises to get disparate creative ideas aligned, set expectations, build trust, and identify the foundational elements of whatever you’ll be building as a team.

Lecture

Poetic Kinetics: Patrick Shearn, Installation Artist

Patrick Shearn is an LA-based artist specializing in larger-than-life, immersive public art installations. Shearn’s expertise in animatronics, robotics and visual effects has lent traction to a prolific career as creative director for his studio Poetic Kinetics. Shearn will walk us through his unconventional career trajectory, offering a behind-the-scenes look at how his art comes to fruition and reimagines the landscapes it inhabits.
